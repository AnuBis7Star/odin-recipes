Caramelized onion pasta

Recipe website.

Delicious caramelized onion pasta for everyone (that likes onion...)

This caramelized onion pasta is a new and really different pasta dish that just
might become your new favorite. Sun-dried tomatoes and chili onion crunch
add a new dimension to rich, silky caramelized onions, and the result is both
elegant and worth the effort.

Ingredients:
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, divided
  • 3 large onions, halved and thinly sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • ¼ cup matchstick-cut sun-dried tomatoes
  • 1 ½ tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 ½ tablespoons chili onion crunch (such as Trader Joe’s®) or more to taste
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 8 ounces spaghetti
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley. or as needed for garnish
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese, or as needed for garnish
Steps:
  1. Heat olive oil and 1 tablespoon butter in a large skillet over medium-low heat.
    Stir in onions until well coated. Cook, stirring occasionally, until they start to
    caramelize and turn golden brown, 20 to 25 minutes. You can add water,
    1 tablespoon at a time, as onion mixture cooks to speed up caramelization
    and keep onions moist.
  2. Add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute. Stir in sun-dried tomatoes,
    soy sauce, chili onion crunch, and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Meanwhile, bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
    Cook spaghetti in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm
    to the bite, about 12 minutes. Drain, reserving 1/2 cup pasta water.
  4. Transfer pasta to the skillet with caramelized onions. Toss everything together
    until well coated, and stir in remaining tablespoon of butter. Add reserved pasta
    water, if needed, a tablespoon at a time.
  5. Garnish with chopped parsley and Parmesan cheese, and serve.
